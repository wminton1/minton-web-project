Latest Star Wars News

10.29.25 - Star Wars Releases a new Season of Star Wars: Visions

Star Wars: Visions Season three picks up where some of the stories in Season 2 left off as well as delves into some new storylines. It's available now on Disney+.

10.28.25 - Ahsoka Season 2 Progress

According to reporting, Ahsoka Season 2 has wrapped filming and the episodes are set to be released in 2026.
Season 2 will most likely show more Hayden Christensen(Anakin Skywalker) as well as contain a lot more
action from additional characters from other series.

10.27.25 - Fans showing positivity for Baylon Skoll Recast after passing of Ray Stevenson

With the unfortunate passing of Ray Stevenson last year came the loss of one of the main characters of the Ahsoka:
Baylon Skoll. Fans, while saddened by the loss of Stevenson, show excitement with the recast Rory McCann. McCann, known for his
role in Game of Thrones, also expressed his condolences and is happy to fill the role.
