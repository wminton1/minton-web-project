[Spoiler Alert] Definitive Order to Watch Star Wars
Episode Number Title Synopsis
1 The Phantom Menace - 1999 The Trade Federation blockade the planet Naboo and two Jedi are dispatched to settle the dispute. After negotiations fall through, it becomes clear that the Trade Federation intend to circumvent Republic Law. The Jedi, now on Naboo's surface, need to alert the Naboo Parliament of these developments and take them back to the galactic capitol to plea for the Republic's assistance.
2 Attack of the Clones - 2002 Senator Amidala, former Queen of Naboo, has become the target of political violence. In light of this, the Jedi order has dispatched Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi and his Padawan, Anakin Skywalker. As attacks against the Senator increase the Jedi suggest she return to her home planet of Naboo under the watch of Anakin while Obi-Wan further investigates the assassination attempts. While in hiding, Anakin must face a personal battle... alone.
3 Revenge of the Sith - 2005 After three years of a raging galactic war, Knight Anakin Skywalker and Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi are dispatched to rescue the chancellor of the Republic, Sheev Palpatine after being captured by General Grievous. After his rescue, the Jedi become suspicious of the chancellor as he seems to have formed a close bond to Anakin. After being promoted to council member, the order tasks Anakin to spy on the chancellor and Anakin, infuriated by this, embraces the Dark Side.
4 A New Hope - 1977 The Galactic Empire, under order of Darth Vader, boards Senator Leia Organa's imperial corvette, the Tantive IV, and arrerst her on the suspicions of possession of inforation crucial to an Imperial Project. Two of her droids escape the vessel on to the planet Tatooine below. The droids, C-3PO and R2-D2, are captured by junkers and sold to someone who would prove to be the biggest existential threat to the Empire.
5 The Empire Strikes Back - 1980 Imperial Forces invade the planet Hoth, home to one of several prominent Rebel Bases in an effort to capture Leia, Han and Luke. Darth Vader and his forces lay siege to the planet but Han, Leia, and Chewbacca escape on the Millenium Falcon while Luke makes his own escape in his X-Wing. Luke returns to Dagobah to continue his training with Yoda, while Han and Leia make their way to an old friend to lay low.
6 Return of the Jedi - 1983 The Galactic Civil War is coming to an end. Luke returns to Tatooine to rescue his friends, Leia Organa, Han Solo, and Chewbacca, from Jabba the Hutt. After making their escape, Luke makes his way back to Dagobah one last time for guidance from Yoda. Han, Leia, and Chewbacca prepare for a final battle with the Empire.
